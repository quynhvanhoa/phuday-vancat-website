How to find us

The temple is about 100 km south of Hà Nội and 35 km from Ninh Bình city

[Google Maps — Phủ Dầy Vân Cát]

Directions

From Hà Nội: Take the Pháp Vân — Cầu Giẽ — Ninh Bình expressway, exit at Cao Bồ junction, follow Highway 10 toward Nam Định, pass Gôi town, then turn into Kim Thái Commune.

From Ninh Bình: Follow Highway 10 toward Nam Định, pass Gôi town, then turn into Kim Thái Commune.

Transport: Accessible by private car, motorbike, intercity bus, or coach.

Support our heritage

Every contribution helps us preserve, restore, and pass on the heritage of Phủ Dầy Vân Cát to future generations. The Holy Mother witnesses every sincere heart.

You can make an offering directly at the temple or contact us in advance through our official channels.

Contact us to contribute Call directly

Ways to contribute

  • Direct offering: At the temple's offering box, with receipts on request.
  • Bank transfer: Please contact us directly for official account details.
  • Material gifts: Restoration materials, ritual items, incense — please notify the temple in advance.
  • Volunteering: Participate in restoration and maintenance work during festival seasons.

We sincerely thank every contributor and pray that the Holy Mother bestows her blessings upon you and your family.
