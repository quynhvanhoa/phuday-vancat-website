About this area

The Three-Entrance Gate

The Three-Entrance Gate is the sacred threshold leading into the temple. Its curved roofs, intricately carved eaves, and dragon-phoenix motifs represent the pinnacle of traditional Vietnamese architectural art.

Two ancient Hán–Nôm parallel sentences flank the gate, praising the virtues of Holy Mother Liễu Hạnh and offering prayers for the peace of the nation and the prosperity of the people.
